God of war Ragnorak is about the adventure of two characters name Kratos and Atreus. In this game Atreus tries to find who he is and at the same time tries to be more independent without his fathers guidence. All in all this game gives a younger audience about father and son relationship.

characters

Kratos is a spartan warrior who was once the God of War in ancient greek mythology. >
In this game he tries to leave his violent past behind him and is now a father to Atreus and seeks to guide him while dealing whit his inner turmoil. Another Important character in this game is Atreus who is curios and intelligent boy, has deep connection to Norse mythology
in this game he learns about his divine heritage and develops his own set of powers and abilities.
As he finds out about his identity as Loki,the trickster god he is eager to find out more about his heritage in this game.
